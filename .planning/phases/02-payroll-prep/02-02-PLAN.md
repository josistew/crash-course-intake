From 02-RESEARCH.md — Overtime-Tracker layout:
```
Col A: Employee Name
Col B-H: Mon-Sun Hours (SUMIFS by employee + specific date)
Col I: Weekly Total (=SUM(B:H))
Col J: Overtime Hours (=MAX(I-40, 0))
Col K: Regular Hours (=MIN(I, 40))
Col L: Status (=IF(I>=38,"HIGH",IF(I>=32,"WARN","OK")))
```

From 02-RESEARCH.md — Payroll-Output layout:
```
Col A: Employee Name (='Overtime-Tracker'!A2)
Col B: Regular Hours (='Overtime-Tracker'!K2)
Col C: Overtime Hours (='Overtime-Tracker'!J2)
Col D: Current Pay Rate (=IFERROR(VLOOKUP(A2,'Employee-Roster'!B:D,3,FALSE),"RATE NOT FOUND"))
Col E: Estimated Gross Pay (=(B2*D2)+(C2*D2*1.5))
Col F: Pay Period (named cell showing week ending date)
```

CRITICAL anti-patterns from research:
- Do NOT use Location as a SUMIFS criterion in the Overtime-Tracker weekly total — aggregate across ALL locations per employee (PAY-04)
- Use SUMIFS (not VLOOKUP) for hours aggregation — handles multiple rows per shift
- VLOOKUP for pay rate must use Employee-Roster column B (Square Name), not column A (Display Name)
- Week start must be a named cell (manually set), NOT TODAY()
